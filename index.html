Who We Are

We are a group of individuals passionate about collecting scrap metals.

Ewing Metals is Canadian company which provide household and commercial scap metal pickup free of charge in the Toronto and surrounding areas.We works with people, government organizations and organizations in the elimination of scrap metal. Scrap Metals we reuse, process and grade a wide range of scrap metal to help keep our condition clean. We deal in copper, insulated copper wire and copper bearing materials. We gather your old materials and reuse them in a supportable and reusable way.

What We Do

We help in creating, advancing and operating various scrap metal cleanups throughout Toronto and nearby areas. From development garbage to white merchandise, we will pull away any ferrous or non-ferrous metals. Our experienced staff will go to your area, examine your present scrap program and prescribe a tweaked arrangement custom fitted to address your issues. Our programs offer a helpful and productive scrap evacuation system.

Industrial Scrap

We deal in mechanical scrap, steel, aluminum, building scrap, and so on.

Automative Scrap

We are searching for motors, radiators, transmissions, differentials and other automative scrap

Household Scrap

We collect all household scrap, including clothes washers, stoves and window outlines.
